WIPULSCAN PRO – Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Overview

WIPULSCAN PRO ("the App") is a WiFi signal scanner and AR heatmap visualizer developed by Cobra Dynamics. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data.

2. Data Collection

The App collects the following data locally on your device only:

  • WiFi Signal Data: Signal strength (RSSI/dBm), connection type, network speed, and latency measurements of your currently connected WiFi network.
  • Device Sensors: Gyroscope and accelerometer data for movement tracking and AR visualization.
  • Camera Feed: Used in real-time for AR overlay. Camera frames are processed locally and never stored or transmitted.

3. Data Storage

All measurement data is stored exclusively on your device using local storage. No data is transmitted to any server. No cloud storage is used. No analytics or tracking services are integrated.

4. No Personal Data

The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). We do not collect names, email addresses, location coordinates, IP addresses, or any other personal data.

5. Permissions

  • Camera: Required for AR visualization overlay. Frames are processed locally only.
  • WiFi/Network: Required to measure signal strength and connection quality.
  • Location (Android): Required by Android OS to access WiFi scan results. We do not store or transmit location data.
  • Motion Sensors: Required for gyroscope-based movement tracking in AR mode.

6. Third-Party Services

The App performs network speed measurements by downloading small test files from Cloudflare's public CDN (speed.cloudflare.com). No personal data is transmitted during these tests. No other third-party services are used.

7. In-App Purchases

The App offers a one-time Pro upgrade purchase. Purchase transactions are handled entirely by Google Play Store or Apple App Store. We do not have access to your payment information.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. GDPR Compliance

For users in the European Economic Area: Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, the App is fully GDPR-compliant. All data remains on your device under your control.

10. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the App and on this page.
