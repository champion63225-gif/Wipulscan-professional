## Full Description
**See your WiFi signal – in Augmented Reality.**

WIPULSCAN PRO transforms your smartphone into a professional WiFi signal scanner with real-time AR visualization. Walk through your home or office and watch colored 3D clouds appear in your camera view:

🟢 **Green** = Strong signal (excellent quality)
🟡 **Yellow** = Good signal (stable connection)
🟠 **Orange** = Medium signal (usable but limited)
🔴 **Red** = Weak signal (poor coverage)

### How It Works
1. Open the app and grant camera + sensor permissions
2. Tap "Start Scan"
3. Walk slowly through your space
4. Watch colored clouds appear where you measured
5. Find dead zones and optimize your router position
